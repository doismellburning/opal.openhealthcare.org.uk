Infection

In partnership with the Hospital for Tropical Diseases and UCLH we have developed elCID - the electronic Clinical Infection Database. elCID is a specialist implementation of OPAL for Infection doctors focussed on their clinical needs.

Featuring rich data capture and audit functionality, management of consultations in an MDT setting, along with clinically validated pathways and interfaces, elCID can help your Infection unit today.
